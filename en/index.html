Our Story

An evening in the city
worth staying for

D Town by A Train is a creative gastropub in Taipei's Da'an District, part of the "Alphabet Series" brand (A Train, B Line, C Park, D Town, E Village, F Gallery, U Square — seven venues in total), located on the second floor of a lane beside SOGO Zhongxiao, about a 1-minute walk from MRT Zhongxiao Fuxing Station Exit 4. One of the largest bistro & cocktail bars in Taipei's East District with a retro British aesthetic, specialising in handcrafted cocktails (adjustable sweetness, strength and acidity), Japanese wagyu, charcoal-grilled skewers, oysters, and the Tea Highball series available in 11 flavours. Minimum NT$600 per person (+10% service charge), open Sunday–Thursday 18:00–01:30, Friday–Saturday 18:00–02:30, accommodating 1–10 guests. Reserve by calling 02-2721-2772 after 4 PM or via Inline online booking. Good Food. Good Wine. Good Times.

You need a drink, because you have something to say. No special occasion required — just an evening to eat well, share a drink, and finally say what's on your mind. Push open the second-floor door and you'll find a retro-British space, one of the largest bistro & cocktail bars in the East District: dim yet open, with a quiet corner waiting just for you.

From MRT Zhongxiao Fuxing Exit 4, it's a one-minute walk — look up for the lights on the second floor.

FAQ

The questions first-timers ask most

We've gathered the details guests care about most, so you don't have to keep scrolling.

Where is D Town by A Train located?
2F, No. 2, Lane 49, Sec. 4, Zhongxiao E. Rd., Da'an Dist., Taipei. It's about a 1-minute walk from MRT Zhongxiao Fuxing Station Exit 4.
Where is the entrance?
Facing Qmomo, look to your right for the small door next to the street lamp. Take the elevator up to the 2nd floor and you're here.
What are the opening hours?
Sunday to Thursday 18:00–01:30, Friday and Saturday 18:00–02:30.
What is the minimum charge?
There is a minimum charge of NT$600 per person, plus a 10% service charge.
How can I make a reservation?
Call us at 02-2721-2772 after 4 PM, or book online through Inline.
Is there parking available?
We don't have our own parking, so we recommend taking the MRT (about a 1-minute walk from Zhongxiao Fuxing Station Exit 4). If you're driving, you can park at the nearby Kimpton Da An Hotel car park, about a 3-minute walk away.
Are pets allowed?
Furry friends are welcome! To keep the dining environment comfortable for everyone, please bring them in a pet stroller or carrier where possible. Thank you for your cooperation.
Do you host private events and gatherings?
We welcome enquiries for small private buyouts and private dining. For details, please call us at 02-2721-2772 after 4 PM.
How many people is it suited for, and can you seat large tables?
Whether you're coming solo for a little me time over a drink, sharing a quiet round for two, or gathering 6–10 friends or colleagues, it's a great fit. For large tables or bigger groups, we recommend booking ahead by phone or via Inline so we can arrange the right seating for you.
How big is the venue and how many people can it hold?
D Town by A Train is one of the largest bistro & cocktail bars in Taipei's East District — retro-British in style with generous, spacious seating that suits everything from a table for two to large group tables, making it ideal for group dining and private buyouts. For exact capacity and buyout options, please call us at 02-2721-2772 after 4 PM.
Can I host a birthday celebration or group party?
Absolutely! Birthday celebrations, group parties and private dinners are all welcome — we can arrange seating, cocktails and food to match your party size. Call us at 02-2721-2772 after 4 PM and let's plan it together.
Are there vegetarian or special dietary options?
Our menu centres on meat and seafood dishes, so vegetarian options are limited. If you have special dietary needs, we recommend letting us know by phone when you book, and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Can I customise the sweetness or alcohol level of my cocktail?
Yes. All handcrafted cocktails can be adjusted for sweetness, strength and acidity to match your taste. Not sure what to order? Just tell the staff what style you enjoy and they'll recommend something.
Is D Town the same restaurant as A Train?
They're not the same venue, but they're sister restaurants under the same brand group. The Alphabet Series currently has seven locations: A Train, B Line by A Train, C Park by A Train, D Town by A Train, E Village by A Train, F Gallery by A Train, and U Square by A Train — each independently operated with its own style. D Town by A Train at Zhongxiao Fuxing is the largest of the group.
Can I bring my own birthday cake?
You're welcome to bring a birthday cake — no cake-cutting fee applies. If you have special birthday arrangements in mind, feel free to mention it when you reserve.
Are guests under 18 allowed?
D Town by A Train is a gastropub. By law, alcohol cannot be served to guests under 18. Minors are welcome to dine alongside adults and enjoy non-alcoholic drinks and food.
What flavours does the Tea Highball come in?
The Tea Highball series pairs Taiwanese specialty teas with Japanese shochu or plum wine, across 11 flavours: Kishu Plum Wine, Oita Wheat Shochu, Miyazaki Earl Grey Shochu, Huatan Jasmine, Mingjian Four Seasons Oolong, Uji Sencha, Luye Honey-Scented Amber, Yuchi Ruby Black Tea, Emei Oriental Beauty, Lugu Roasted Oolong, and Muzha Tieguanyin.
Can I walk in without a reservation?
Yes, but Friday and Saturday nights and busy holiday periods fill up quickly. To guarantee a seat, call after 4 PM at 02-2721-2772 or book online via Inline. Walk-in availability depends on the day.
How far in advance should I book?
For weekdays, 2–3 days ahead is usually enough. For weekends, 4–6 days is recommended. Birthday celebrations or groups of 6 or more should book earlier. Call after 4 PM at 02-2721-2772 or use Inline online booking.
Is D Town good for a date?
Yes. The lighting is dim, the space is large enough to keep things quiet, and two-person tables have their own corner feel. If you're not sure what to order, just tell the staff what you enjoy — cocktails can be adjusted for sweetness and strength, so there's no wrong choice.
Is there a private room?
There's no separate private room, but the space is large with distinct seating zones. If you have specific seating needs or are interested in a full buyout, call after 4 PM at 02-2721-2772 to discuss.
